About Phoemar

Welcome to Phoemar! We are a sustainable farm located in Kathiani, Kitui West Subcounty, where we prioritize fresh, quality produce and responsible farming practices.

Our Journey

Founded in 2022, Phoemar has rapidly grown into a farm dedicated to producing high-quality, organic crops and livestock. Our farm is located in the heart of Kathiani, where we harness the natural environment to bring you the best of what nature has to offer.

Our Products

At Phoemar, we offer a variety of products grown and produced with care. Our diverse selection includes:

Onions

Fresh, organic onions grown with the best farming practices, delivering great taste and quality.

Watermelon

Sweet and juicy watermelons, grown under natural conditions to ensure top-notch flavor and nutrition.

Tomatoes

Ripe, fresh tomatoes that are perfect for cooking or enjoying raw in your favorite salads.

Honey

Pure, natural honey harvested directly from our beehives, with no additives or preservatives.

Chicken

Free-range, healthy chickens raised in a clean, safe environment, offering the best poultry products.

Fish

Fresh, farm-raised fish from our well-maintained fishponds, offering a healthy protein source.

Our Location

Phoemar is located in Kathiani, Kitui West Subcounty, where we take advantage of the fertile land and favorable climate to produce our crops and livestock. Our farm is easily accessible, and we invite you to visit us to experience the beauty of sustainable farming.
